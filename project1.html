Usertesting

Usertesting hired me to work on their Asset Toolkit, a code based styleguide that helps developers keep component styles consistent across their product.

User Interface Design | User Exeperience

About the Project

As the UserTesting platform grew, many of the product's styles began to look different across various plaforms. On the frontend engineering team, the problem I was trying to solve is how to unify styles across the product to lessen this dilema from happening.

My Approach

Research was key in this project. Using the UserTesting platforn, i tested other styleguides to understand what key aspects needed to be present and which layouts and navigation styles developers and designers prefered. I came up with a few wireframes that represented the most liked navigation style.

Exploration

I explored various directions for each component based on how easy it was for a developer to access the acces the infomation they were looking
