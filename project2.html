AdsNative Hackathon:
Redesigning Spotify's Ad Experience

At the AdsNative Hackthon, I chose to hack Spotify's ad experience to make ads less intrustive and more targeted to the user.

User Interface Design | User Exeperience

Intial Research

Through running user research studies, I was able to determine what users disliked and liked users disliked about the ad experience. Many though that the testers felt the ads were "intrusive" and "too flashy".

How do we build better ad experience?

Placement is key. The more the ads is seemlessly in the interface, the more enjoyabke the experience will be. Working adds into layouts that already present was the key to solving this problem
