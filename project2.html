Shout!

Android Studio, Java, XML, Photoshop


Shout was an idea that I joined in on at a Hackathon. It is a way for college students to get information and notifications on campus events. It utilizes iBeacon and Bluetooth technologizes to send push notifications to a user about campus events in the area. Once a user walks in range of an iBeacon, a push notification is sent to their phone to notify them about a specific campus event.

This app won 3nd place at the Hackathon.
