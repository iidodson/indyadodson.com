AdsNative Hackathon:
Redesigning Spotify's Ad Experience

At the AdsNative Hacktahon, I chose to hack Spotify's ad experience to make ads less intrusive and more targeted to each user.

User Interface Design | User Exeperience

Initial Research

Through user research studies, I was able to determine what dislikes and likes users had about the current ad experience. The general consensus was that testers felt the ads were "intrusive" and "too flashy".

How do We Build Better Ad Experiences?

There is always a trade off with ads. Making the ads very intrusive and flashy can often give users a reason to buy pro features while it can also deter them from using the product. With only 30% of Spotify users paying for premium, it seems like the intrusiveness of the ads are intentional. Regardless, the placement could still be improved. The more the ads are seamlessly integrated into the current layout, the more enjoyable the experience can be and this was key to solving this problem.
