HBU Hackathon:
Quick Gift

At the HBCU Hackathon, I created an app called Quick Gift. This app helps people find gifts for someone special based on gender, relationship, and the occasion.

Graphic Design | Android App Development

About the Project

I focused more on implementation of the product in this project. How would this app be useful to others and what AI could be implemeted to make it as useful as possinle. I started out making a quick logo design and landing page to get the initial concept down.

Nitty Gritty

Focusing less on the design and more on the implementation, I used the Amazon web services API to pull products from the Amazon database based on the criteria users gave such as the gender, relationship, and the occasion.

And so on

While QuickGift won 2nd place at the hackathon. it still needs some design love before its ready to be released. It's a project that I'll definitely come back to so stay tuned.
